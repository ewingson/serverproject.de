now

(indian summer 2025, as of today, Sunday, 2025-10-19)

I am living in BeWo in Herford and have neither partner nor kids. Usually I am busy driving my bike, calculating print orders or packaging leather. I am living amongst 16 or 17 moreorless somehow active neighbours. Without my linux machine I feel like kindof detox. Our ecosystem can be found here. But - believe me - joy is very rare. It happens when I am listening to The Feelies, Ween, Radio Birdman, Ed Kuepper, Motorpsycho, Yo La Tengo, Soundgarden, Louis Tillet and Charlie Owen, Wake Ooloo, Wayne Kramer, Leaving Trains, H.P. Zinker or Velvet Underground.

Seen from the physical side I am well.
Seen from the financial side I go with Sylvia Juncosa.

I am dreaming of a life, that's worth it. Here, seen from here, the last 15 years were at least a starting point.
